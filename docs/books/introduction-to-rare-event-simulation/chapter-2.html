2.3 Markov Chain Monte Carlo

  • One runs an MCMC chain and samples it from time to time.
  • If the chain has reached equilibrium, we can think of the samples as being from $\pi$, the stationary distribution.
  • If you wait sufficient time between samples, you can view the samples as independent.
  • Overview of Metropolis algorithm.
  • Review of Barker’s algorithm.
  • Review of Gibb’s sampler on $\Lambda$-valued process.
    • $\Lambda$-valued processes are usually colors, gray levels, spin states, etc.
