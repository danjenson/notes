Data Augmentation and Latent Variables

  • This lecture was a collection of examples that were provided as typed pdf.
  • The key idea was when you are trying to augment your dataset, introduce a latent variable that makes it easier to calculate your parameter $\theta$. Then iterate (1) sampling that latent variable conditional on your data and the parameters and (2) sampling the parameters based on the data and latent variable.
  • One nice property to look for is data independence given your parameters.
  • There is an extended example about protein binding sites in the pdf.
